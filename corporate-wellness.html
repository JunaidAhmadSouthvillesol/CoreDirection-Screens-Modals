How to earn points

By downloading our app and connecting your mobile phone or smart watch our technology will reward points for 5 key behaviors.

  • - Walk to earn Step Points
  • - Move to collect Active Heart Rate Points
  • - Watch Virtual content for On-Demand Points
  • - Book & Join listed activity for Check-in Points
  • - Schedule your wellness for Activity Points

Core Points Explained

Core Points

The highest points earned against one of five categories (Activity log, Check-ins, Steps, Heart rate, and content consumed) within 24-hour period are considered core points, up to a maximum of 10 points per day.
